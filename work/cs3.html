A.I. for Good Simulator

Overview

A.I. for Good Simulator is a web tool for NGOs and local authorities to model potential COVID-19 outbreaks inside refugee settlements. The goal of the simulator is to assist these organizations in preparing and responding to COVID-19 and future epidemics.

Type of Work

User Surveys, User Interviews, Competitive Analysis, Documentation, Wireframes

Duration

3 months

Organization

A.I. for Good U.K.

Team

Data Modeling Team, NGO Professionals

Problem

7 million people are in refugee-like situations living in camps. Refugee camps around the world lack the information necessary to support response planning and advocacy efforts to acquire life-saving medical resources. Health agencies and universities are focused on modeling the impact of COVID-19 on a global and national level.

Solution

Modeling the spread of COVID-19 within refugee settlements and creating an interactive web tool for NGO professionals to predict the possible spread of COVID-19 to support them in the areas below.

Essential Supply Forecasting

Serve as a tool for NGOs and grassroots organizations to mobilize action and coordinate efforts to slow down the spread of COVID-19.

Inform Policy Making

Identify the most effective COVID-19 interventions designed for refugee camps and help reduce the spread and avoid severe casualties.
