DailyPay Quiz

Overview

I collaborated with the Product Marketing team to build a quiz experience that educates prospects and existing partners about the real costs of different on-demand pay experiences. DailyPay enables major employers to provide on-demand pay to their employees, which gives them up to 100% access to their earned income and help them improve their financial health. This project is password-protected.

Type of Work

Heuristic Evaluation, Competitor Research, Stakeholder Interviews, Wireframing, UI Design

Duration

2 weeks

Team
