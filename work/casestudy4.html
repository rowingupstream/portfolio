Product Marketing Quiz

DailyPay is a fintech company that provides earned income software and a suite of payroll products that enable large employers to provide hourly workers with flexible access to their pay.

I collaborated with the Product Marketing team to build a quiz experience that educates prospects and partners about the hidden costs of different on-demand pay experiences. DailyPay enables major employers to provide on-demand pay to their employees, which gives them up to 100% access to their earned income and helps them improve their financial well-being.
