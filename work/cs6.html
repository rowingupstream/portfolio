REI Co-op

Overview

REI Co-op believes a life outdoors is a life well-lived. To help them carry out their mission we brought their “Find Out” campaign to life in the San Francisco, Boston and Atlanta. "Find Out" celebrated curiosity and the search for something more—with answers and experiences that can only be found outdoors. We created pop-up activations and took part in local community events like outdoor movie nights, yoga, and dog park meetups that helped REI connect with its members and local residents in an authentic way.
